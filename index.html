HOW IT WORKS
Uses your phone's ARCore tracking — no ruler, no calibration needed.

1. Start AR session
2. Hold phone below right eye — tap Mark R
3. Move to left eye — tap Mark L
4. PD = real 3D distance between marks

Phone must stay level — only lateral (horizontal) distance is used.
